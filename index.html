What is MoonSweep

In the future, a new moon race is taking place, and this time private companies will play key roles in creating lunar infrastructures.  In this new lunar economy geographical, topological and geological information will be crucial in order to face the harsh environment. Our goal is to collect, process and provide these informations in a clear way to our customers. 
This will favour a safer and more aware development of human activities on the moon.
